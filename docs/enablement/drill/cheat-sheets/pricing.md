# Pricing Card

Full playbook in `04-pricing.md`. Glance version.

## The three tiers

| Tier | Price | Shape |
|---|---|---|
| **Pilot** | $50-75K fixed | 4-6 wks, 1 endpoint, credits 100% to year 1 |
| **Mission** | $150-250K/yr + $50-100K impl | mid-market, 1-3 endpoints, ~10M queries/mo |
| **Constellation** | $400K-$1M+/yr | multi-BU, multi-env, air-gap, dedicated TAM |

## Anchoring rules

- Anchor high. Quote list. Let procurement discount you.
- Don't price per query. Platform + envelope.
- Implementation is a feature, bundled visibly. Not a separate tax.
- Price like deception (Acalvio), not like an API filter (Lakera).
- Discount on services/year-1, never on platform list. Protect the renewal.

## Pushback → response

| They say | You say |
|---|---|
| "Expensive." | "Compared to what?" (let them anchor first) |
| "Lakera is $75K." | "API filter pricing. We're a deception platform with intel output. Different category." |
| "No budget this year." | "When does your fiscal year open?" |
| "Smaller pilot?" | "Pilot's already sized for one endpoint, 6 weeks. What's driving smaller scope?" |
| "We'll build it." | "12-18 months of engineering. What's that worth?" |

## The discovery questions that size a deal

Endpoints? Query volume? Topology (VPC/hybrid/air-gap)? Cloud(s)? SIEM count? Industry rule packs? Residency? SLA? Downstream bridges? Co-managed SOC? (Full list in `04-pricing.md`.)
