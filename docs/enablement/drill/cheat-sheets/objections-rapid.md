# Objections Rapid-Fire

The one-line version. Full answers and pressure-handling in `08-objections-and-answers.md`. Drill these until they're reflexes.

## The protocol (3 reflexes)

1. Pause. Two sentences. Stop.
2. Agree with the true part, then pivot.
3. Don't know? Bridge to what you do know. Defer with a date. Never invent.

## Rapid-fire (objection → core answer)

- **"98.5% of what?"** → 66 of 67 attack turns. Raw rate on all 87 is ~76%. I give you the attack number because it's the meaningful one. (Volunteer the 76%.)
- **"Numbers seem inflated."** → Measured, reproducible campaign. Watch what we DON'T claim: no prod numbers, no certs we lack.
- **"You have no customers."** → Right, lighthouse phase. Our attention, our roadmap, lighthouse pricing. Price it as access.
- **"Why not Lakera?"** → Keep it. We catch what beats it. EchoLeak proved filters fail. Prevention + detection.
- **"Just a honeypot?"** → Same proven idea, new surface. And it engages + emits intel, not just sits there.
- **"Acalvio does AI deception."** → They make a honeypot WITH AI. We make one that IS an AI.
- **"Who else does this?"** → No commercial product we've found. Get in early, shape the category.
- **"Add latency?"** → ~30ms on clean traffic. Model takes hundreds of ms. Real model never sees attackers.
- **"FP rate in prod?"** → Zero on test set, no prod yet (honest). Structural advantage: legit users never touch the decoy.
- **"Realizes it's a decoy?"** → By then they've revealed technique, tooling, intent. Win. GhostCrew keeps decoys looking live.
- **"Avoid the decoy?"** → Routing logic isn't exposed. To map it they have to probe, which is captured signal.
- **"See our real data?"** → Decoy isn't wired to real systems, leaks nothing. Benign traffic passes through. Deploy in your VPC.
- **"ML always on?"** → Ships in the bundle. Production bundle = always on. We added a flag to fail closed if it's missing.
- **"Expensive."** → Compared to what? (Let them anchor.) Deception pricing, not filter pricing.
- **"Build it ourselves."** → 12-18 months of engineering. What's that worth, plus exposure while you build?
- **"No budget."** → When does your fiscal year open?
- **"SOC 2 / FedRAMP?"** → Not yet, won't pretend. We support your program with evidence, can produce a mapping package.
- **"Trust an early vendor?"** → Signed, reproducible bundles you verify yourself. Source-available runtime. Standard STIX. No black box.
- **"If you go under?"** → Source-available core, standard output format. Betting early isn't betting your security on us.
- **"How deep is ADLibs really?"** → Generates AD deception objects + detection today. Live AD wiring is deployment scoping. (Hold the honest line.)

## The "I don't know" script

"Good question, I don't want to guess. What I can tell you is [closest thing]. Let me get you the precise answer by [day]." Then follow up.
